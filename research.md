## üè¢ Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

#### 1. **Bilt Rewards** (Closest competitor)
- **Focus**: Receipt tracking + rewards
- **Strengths**: Banking integration, rewards system
- **Weaknesses**: Limited warranty focus, no AI extraction
- **Market Position**: $200M valuation, 500K+ users

#### 2. **Everdence** (Receipt management)
- **Focus**: Digital receipt storage
- **Strengths**: Email integration, simple UI
- **Weaknesses**: No warranty tracking, basic features
- **Market Position**: Small player, limited funding

#### 3. **WarrantyLife** (Basic warranty tracking)
- **Focus**: Manual warranty entry
- **Strengths**: Simple warranty database
- **Weaknesses**: No AI, manual entry only, outdated UI
- **Market Position**: Legacy solution, declining user base

### Indirect Competitors

#### 1. **Apple Wallet / Google Pay** (Digital receipts)
- **Threat Level**: Medium
- **Gap**: No warranty extraction or reminders

#### 2. **Notion / Airtable** (Manual tracking)
- **Threat Level**: Low
- **Gap**: No automation, requires technical setup

#### 3. **Manufacturer Apps** (Brand-specific)
- **Threat Level**: Medium
- **Gap**: Fragmented, single-brand only
